💡 Why Split? Most logical Croatian beach base — direct bus & flight from Budapest, gateway to Hvar/Brač islands, walkable old town (Diocletian's Palace), great beaches (Bačvice, Kašjuni), and ferry hub for day trips. June is ideal: warm sea (~22°C), fewer crowds than July/August.
